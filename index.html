About

CARD Entertainment of Hamilton, NJ is a mobile DJ service that strives to provide a one-of-a-kind, energetic, high class experience to take your event to the next level.

No matter what your event is, our experienced DJ and MC will help make your event a success.

CARD Entertainment provides both music and lighting services for your event. We can also provide uplighting for your venue.

From small and intimate gatherings to large parties, we have the experience to entertain everyone.
